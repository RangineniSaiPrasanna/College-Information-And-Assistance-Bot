    About Us



    Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies - Ongole Campus (RGUKT Ongole) is a premier autonomous institution established by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to offer high-quality education to rural and meritorious students. Founded in 2016, it is one of the four campuses of RGUKT in Andhra Pradesh.

    The institute offers a 6-Year Integrated B.Tech Program, uniquely designed for students completing their 10th class (SSC). This program combines two years of Pre-University Course (PUC) with four years of Engineering education, nurturing students from the foundation level to advanced technical expertise.

    Located in Ongole, Prakasam District, the campus is fully residential and provides a learner-centric environment with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, digital classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, hostels, and a dedicated mess facility.â€™.

    The vision of RGUKT Ongole is to empower rural youth by providing quality technical education and bridging the urban-rural educational gap. It aims to produce socially responsible engineers equipped with strong academic foundations, ethical values, and leadership skills. The institute focuses on holistic development, preparing students to tackle real-world challenges with innovation and integrity.

    TRGUKT Ongole offers a unique and innovative 6-Year Integrated B.Tech Program, designed specifically for students who have completed their 10th standard (SSC). The program consists of two phases: the first two years serve as a Pre-University Course (PUC), focusing on foundational subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, and Life Skills. This is followed by four years of engineering education, where students can specialize in core engineering disciplines. The available branches include Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), Civil Engineering (CE), and Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE). The integrated curriculum ensures that students are well-prepared both academically and practically, with access to digital classrooms, hands-on laboratory experience, and project-based learning. The program is fully residential, promoting a vibrant learning community and fostering collaboration among peers from diverse rural backgrounds.
